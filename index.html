Hi, I am Ashvala Vinay

I am a PhD student at Georgia Tech's Center for Music Technology. I am currently working with Prof. Grace Leslie and Prof. Alexander Lerch on problems at the intersection of music, expression, machine learning and informatics.

I graduated from Berklee College of Music in 2016. Following that, I worked on some personal projects and helped get two startups off the ground with their software engineering work. After that, I got my Masters in Music Technology at GTCMT.

On this page, I will be sharing my work, and some of my posts where I will discuss music, technology and my progress through the course of my PhD work.
